Get Started

Want to learn/practice a foreign language in a free, convenient and casual way?

SPiKL is an intra-university language exchange platform that connects people who want to do just that. If you want to learn/practice a foreign language and can offer to help someone else learn/practice your native language, then SPiKL is the right platform for you.


Connect with people who want to learn your language in exchange for theirs with three simple steps:


#1 Create a profile
#2 Get matched and connect
#3 Meet up

There is no set syllabus, no set time and no cost. Forget about the time zone differences, the distance and the money. With SPiKL, we bring world languages to you.


So go on - get cultured, go global.
